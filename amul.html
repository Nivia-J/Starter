For more than 50 years, the Amul girl has been a member of every Indian house-hold. One of the longest running ad campaignes in the world, the amul topicals have continued to relate to the common man and appeal to everyone with it’s amazing graphics and witty taglines.

What makes Amul’s ad game so strong?
